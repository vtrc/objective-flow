# Review Quality

Treat review as a required gate for meaningful work. Do not let a plan, deliverable, decision, or handoff close merely because it looks plausible or because execution finished.

## Hard Gate

Do not approve or close work when material gaps, unsupported claims, missing evidence, or unresolved risks remain.

## Process

1. Restate the objective, intended audience, acceptance criteria, and constraints.
2. Inspect the work and its evidence against each criterion.
3. Identify defects, gaps, unsupported claims, risks, and missing checks.
4. Rank findings by impact and state the required correction or decision.
5. Reassess changed work and record the remaining risk.
6. Approve only when evidence supports the acceptance criteria; otherwise return the work for correction.
7. Hand off to `confirm-completion` when the work is ready to close, `diagnose-cause` for unexplained defects, `choose-option` for unresolved tradeoffs, and `capture-learning` after the outcome is known.
