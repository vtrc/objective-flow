# Decide Deliberately

## Hard Gate

Do not commit to a consequential choice until criteria, tradeoffs, uncertainty, and reversibility are explicit.

## Process

1. State the decision, objective, owner, deadline, and reversibility.
2. Separate facts, assumptions, constraints, and unknowns.
3. Define decision criteria and rank them by importance.
4. Generate credible options, including maintaining the current course when relevant.
5. Compare options against the criteria; expose tradeoffs and uncertainty.
6. Choose a path, record the rationale, and define the next action, contingency, and review point.
7. Hand off to `review-quality` to challenge the recommendation before commitment and again after execution when the result is meaningful. Hand off to `diagnose-cause` when results fail, `capture-learning` after results are known, and `confirm-completion` only after review passes.
