# Shape Approach

Turn an approved approach into an execution-ready shape.

## Hard Gate

Do not plan or execute consequential work until the approach shape is explicit and approved when approval is needed.

## Process

1. Confirm the approved objective, approach, constraints, and success condition.
2. Define what is in scope and what is out of scope.
3. Describe the workflow in ordered steps, including decision points and handoffs where needed.
4. Identify meaningful risks, assumptions, dependencies, and mitigations.
5. Define validation that demonstrates the success condition.
6. Present the shaped approach for confirmation before planning or executing when the scope, trade-offs, or risks are material.
7. Hand off to `plan-work` for multi-step work, `execute-plan` only for small reversible action, or `explore-options` when the shape exposes a weak direction.

Use this format:

```markdown
Objective: ...
Approved approach: ...
Scope: ...
Workflow: ...
Risks and mitigations: ...
Validation: ...
Out of scope: ...
```

Keep the shaped approach proportionate to the objective. Resolve ambiguity that would change execution; leave operational detail to the execution plan.
