# Confirm Completion

## Hard Gate

Do not declare success unless the reviewed result satisfies the original objective, constraints, and promised outputs.

## Process

1. Restate the objective, acceptance criteria, constraints, and promised outputs.
2. Confirm that `review-quality` has passed for meaningful work, or run a lightweight review for trivial, reversible work.
3. Verify each requirement with direct evidence, not intention or partial progress.
4. Confirm that owners, next steps, records, and dependencies are resolved or explicitly transferred.
5. Identify residual risks and ensure they are accepted, mitigated, or assigned.
6. Declare completion only when review and evidence support the objective; otherwise name the remaining work.
7. Hand off to `review-quality` for unmet criteria, `diagnose-cause` for unexpected gaps, `choose-option` for unresolved choices, and `capture-learning` to capture results after closure.
